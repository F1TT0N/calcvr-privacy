Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

CalcVR ("the App") is developed by Anwar Studio Official ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information in connection with the CalcVR application for Meta Quest.

Summary: CalcVR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App works entirely offline with no network access, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not operate any external servers.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. CalcVR does not require account creation, registration, login, or any form of user identification. The App does not access, collect, or process any personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Calculation history or input data

2. Network Access

CalcVR does not require or use an internet connection. The App makes zero network requests. There is no data transmitted from or to the App at any time. The App functions entirely offline.

3. Data Storage

The App does not store any persistent user data. Calculator state (current display value) exists only in memory while the App is running and is cleared when the App is closed. No data is written to device storage.

4. Third-Party Services

CalcVR does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No in-app purchase systems

The App uses only the standard Android system APIs and Meta Quest platform APIs required for displaying a 2D panel application and supporting hand tracking input.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, license, rent, or share any user data with third parties, because we do not collect any user data in the first place.

6. Children's Privacy

CalcVR is suitable for users of all ages. Since the App does not collect any data whatsoever, there are no special privacy concerns regarding children. The App contains no inappropriate content, advertising, or social features.

7. Permissions

CalcVR requests only the minimum permissions required for operation on Meta Quest:

  • Hand Tracking – Used to allow interaction with the calculator via hand gestures. Hand tracking data is processed locally by the Meta Quest system and is not accessed or stored by the App.

The App does not request permissions for camera, microphone, storage, location, or network access.

8. Data Security

Since CalcVR does not collect, store, or transmit any data, there is no user data at risk. The App operates within the Android operating system's sandboxing mechanism on Meta Quest devices.

9. Your Rights

As CalcVR does not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, export, or delete. If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy while using the App, you are welcome to contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
